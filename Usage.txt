Syntax:

StringReplace "string to find" "string to StringReplace" [file] [output]

Example:

StringReplace "1234" "anic17" user.txt Replaced.txt
Will replace the string '1234' to the string 'anic17' in the file 'user.txt'
and then saving the result into 'Replaced.txt'
